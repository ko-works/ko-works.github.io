Summer in Tokyo — Japanese language & culture

We highlight summer programs at Kudan Japanese Language School because that’s when many U.S. high school and college students can travel—and it’s a great introduction to studying in Japan.

Why Kudan

Kudan offers Japanese language education in Tokyo with a range of programs and activities. Course details, dates, and fees change by year; the school’s official site is the source of truth for the latest brochures and application steps.

Visit Kudan Japanese Language School

Why work with KO WORKS

Many students in Europe and North America contact schools directly—and that can work. If you’d rather have a U.S.-based, English-speaking contact on the East Coast who understands both sides, we’re here to:

  • Answer questions in plain English, in a time zone closer to yours
  • Help you compare options and stay on top of deadlines and paperwork
  • Be a steady point of contact while you coordinate with the school

Other programs at Kudan

Kudan also offers short-term courses (often from about two weeks), long-term study (including programs that may require a student visa), and specialty options. We don’t list every track on this site—requirements and availability vary.

If summer isn’t the right fit, or you’re considering a longer stay, reach out. We’ll help you understand next steps and connect you with the right information.
