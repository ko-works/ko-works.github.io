Summer Holiday Course

Each summer, high school and college students from around the world join the Summer Holiday Course at Kudan Institute of Japanese Language & Culture in Tokyo. Over 3 to 7 weeks, students attend daily Japanese classes, take part in cultural workshops, and travel across Japan — including overnight trips to Atami (hot springs by the sea) and Kyoto.

A typical day

  • ▶Morning: Japanese language classes for all levels, from complete beginner to advanced
  • ▶Afternoon: Cultural workshops (tea ceremony, calligraphy, cooking), city excursions, and guided day trips
  • ▶Housing: Homestay with a Japanese host family or student dormitory, arranged by Kudan

About Kudan

Kudan has welcomed international students in central Tokyo for over 35 years. With small class sizes, qualified instructors, and a full calendar of cultural programming, it offers the kind of structured, immersive environment that students thrive in and parents can feel confident about.

The campus

Kudan sits in the Iidabashi / Kudanshita area of central Tokyo — one of the city's best-connected neighborhoods. Multiple subway and JR lines are within a few minutes' walk.

Everything students need is nearby: convenience stores, restaurants, cafés, bookstores, parks, and historic shrines. It's a safe, walkable home base for exploring the rest of Tokyo on weekends.
