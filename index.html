Summer programs

The best place to start

Every summer, high school and college students from around the world head to Tokyo to study Japanese at Kudan. Programs typically run 2–4 weeks between July and August—perfect for a break that's equal parts learning and adventure.

Classes in the morning, cultural activities and day trips in the afternoon. Think: learning to write kanji, then exploring Shibuya with new friends from a dozen countries.

Dates, fees, and availability change each year — check Kudan's site or ask us for the latest info.

The school

Kudan Japanese Language School

Located in central Tokyo, Kudan has been welcoming international students for decades. Small classes, experienced teachers, and a calendar full of cultural activities — from tea ceremony to weekend excursions.

Where you'll study
