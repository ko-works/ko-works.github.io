What we do

Choosing a language school abroad can feel overwhelming—course types, dates, housing, and paperwork all at once. We focus on summer programs for high school and college-age learners (our busiest season), and we’re here in the U.S. to answer questions clearly and help you stay organized.

Interested in short-term, long-term (student visa), or other Kudan offerings? You don’t need to sort it out alone—get in touch and we’ll figure out the right path together.
