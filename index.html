Summer Holiday Course

Study Japanese, experience Japan

Every summer, high school and college students from around the world head to Tokyo for Kudan's Summer Holiday Course — 3 to 7 weeks of Japanese language classes, cultural activities, and real adventure. Depending on the course, you'll also go on overnight trips to places like Atami (hot springs by the sea) and Kyoto (temples, shrines, and history), plus day trips to spots like Fuji-Q Highland.

A typical day

  • ▶Morning: Japanese language classes — all levels, from complete beginner to advanced
  • ▶Afternoon: Cultural activities (tea ceremony, calligraphy, cooking), city excursions, and day trips
  • ▶Housing: Homestay with a Japanese family or dormitory — arranged through Kudan

The school

Kudan Japanese Language School

Located in central Tokyo, Kudan has been welcoming international students for decades. Small classes, experienced teachers, and a calendar full of cultural activities — from tea ceremony to weekend excursions.

The campus

Kudan is located in the Iidabashi / Kudanshita area of central Tokyo — one of the most well-connected neighborhoods in the city. Multiple subway and JR lines are within a few minutes' walk.

The surrounding area has everything you need day-to-day: convenience stores, restaurants, cafés, bookstores, parks, and historic shrines. It's a great home base for exploring the rest of Tokyo on weekends, too.
